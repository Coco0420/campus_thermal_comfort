💻 Online Survey

10–15 minutes · Complete anytime
  • Answer questions about your outdoor thermal comfort
  • Mark the hottest & coldest spots on an interactive campus map
  • Share how weather affects your campus behavior
  • Complete anytime during the collection period

🌳 On-Site Session

30 minutes · Weekdays 12:30–1:00 pm
  • Guided tour of 3 outdoor campus locations
  • Rate your thermal comfort at each spot
  • Researcher records weather conditions simultaneously
  • Max 7 participants per session
💵 $10 gift card — sent to your email within 3–7 business days

Who can participate?

  • Currently enrolled student at University of Wisconsin–Madison or Texas A&M University
  • Undergraduate or graduate student, ages 18–35
  • You may join the online survey only, the on-site session only, or both — each is independent
